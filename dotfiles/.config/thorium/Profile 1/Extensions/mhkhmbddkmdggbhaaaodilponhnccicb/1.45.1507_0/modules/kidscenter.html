What is COPPA?
COPPA stands for the "Children's Online Privacy Protection Act" and in accordance with the US Federal Trade Commission (FTC), YouTube is requiring all channel to designate each video as either 'made for kids' or not.
Failure to set your content appropriately may result in consequences on YouTube or have legal consequences under COPPA and other laws.
Please watch this video and visit links in the footer for more info.
TubeBuddy cannot offer legal advice.
